About Anthony Mallory

Anthony Mallory is a USA Track and Field Certified Coach and ISSA Certified Personal Trainer with over 10 years of experience at premier fitness facilities including Lifetime Fitness and 24-Hour Fitness.

A former track and field competitor specializing in middle-distance events (100m to 10,000m), Anthony brings real-world racing experience to his coaching philosophy. Trained under Olympic coach Scott Simmons and the American Distance Project methodology, he specializes in developing athletes across all performance domains: balance, flexibility, endurance, strength, speed, and power.

Currently pursuing a sub-2:30 marathon while maintaining elite strength benchmarks, Anthony's mission is to become the #1 resource for the Folsom and Sacramento running community, helping athletes of all levels achieve their goals through evidence-based training.
